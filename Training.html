Training and Education

3DWERKZ can help get you started with in-house training on 3D printing and design.

For Businesses and Startups

Outsourcing design has its cost benefits but there’s nothing better than having your designs in-house and close to your chest. Having your own in-house prototyping specialist and 3D printer means no time zone differences, no/ reduced shipping costs, reduced hassles from miscommunication, faster feedback and development time and your ideas stay secure and not wind up being stolen, infringed upon, copied and counterfeited in foreign markets.

The cost to entry for 3D printing is affordable and entry level software is somewhat intuitive but there’s always a need for someone who has walked the path before you. 3DWERKZ can help you get a 3D printer in your business and train an individual willing to learn the art and science of 3D printing and design and help give you a competitive advantage.

For Prosumers and Children

"3D print for a person, happy and content for a day - teach a person to 3D print, 3D print and innovate for a lifetime!" - 3DWERKZ motto

We are passionate about 3D printing and see that 3D printing’s impact on society is just barely being realized. It is a ‘disruptive’ technology and disruption isn’t a bad thing - it’s here so we have to embrace it and design our future with 3D printing in mind.

3DWERKZ can help people of many ages and walks of life to become their own designers and producers which can unleash their potential in so many ways. With the recent push for STEM education, 3D printing is a focal point where children can gather around to learn and become our future multi-disciplined engineers and technicians for the highly technological 21st Century that is rapidly unfolding before our eyes.
